## 1. The opportunity in one paragraph

Austin Grading & Land Management does professional-grade site work — excavation, grading, drainage, retaining walls — and is **NCDOT pre-qualified** with a **NC licensed commercial General Contractor (Intermediate, up to $1.5M/project)** at the helm. There is an open lane: **the professional, locally-owned, licensed-and-prequalified site contractor for Western NC commercial and development work.** AGLM can own it with a real website, a capability statement that proves legitimacy, and a cleaned-up Google presence.

- Full/legal: **Austin Grading and Land Management LLC** (use in legal/footer/cap statement header).

### Color palette (defined here so site + cap statement match)
| Role | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Base (charcoal-black) | `#1A1A1A` | Primary dark background, headers |

| Accent (construction amber) | `#E8801A` | CTAs, key numbers, highlights, icons |

| Text light | `#F5F5F4` | Body on dark |
| Muted | `#A8A29E` | Secondary text on dark |
| Light section bg | `#F5F4F2` | Contrast sections |
| Text dark | `#1A1A1A` | Body on light |

### Typography
- **Headlines:** a heavy, condensed/industrial sans — e.g. **Oswald** or **Archivo** (700/800) for that rugged, utilitarian feel.
- **Body:** **Inter** (400/500) for readability.
- Google Fonts only. No serifs, no script.

### Visual direction
- Heavy, industrial, rugged — mountain-terrain contractor energy.
- Hero = a real job site (dirt, equipment, graded pad) at golden hour, dark overlay for text contrast.
- Use AGLM's **real photos** (see §11). No stock-photo handshakes or generic office shots.
- High contrast, slightly warm/desaturated treatment to unify the inconsistent source photos.

### Voice & copy rules
- Lead with outcomes and capability, not jargon. Plain, confident, no-fluff — like a foreman who knows the work.
- Short sentences. Numbers and specifics over adjectives ("up to $1.5M per project," "NCDOT pre-qualified") beat "high quality."
- Talk to **GCs, developers, and commercial owners** as a peer who can carry a scope — not down to homeowners.
- Banned words: synergy, leverage, holistic, seamless, world-class, cutting-edge.
- Anchor every page on the two credibility facts: **NCDOT pre-qualified** and **NC licensed commercial GC (up to $1.5M).**

## 5. Services (canonical list)

Use this exact set and order across the site, cap statement, and GBP. Descriptions adapted from AGLM's current site copy (which is solid and accurate — keep it).

1. **Excavation & Site Preparation** *(lead service)* — clearing & grubbing, earthwork, cut & fill, undercut excavation, subgrade prep; building pads, roadways, and parking areas graded and compacted to spec.
2. **Grading & Earthwork** — precision grading for proper elevation, slope, crown, and drainage; residential and commercial.
3. **Drainage Solutions** — stormwater management, culvert & pipe (HDPE/RCP) installation, catch basins, drop inlets, swales, riprap, underdrain, erosion control.
4. **Road & Driveway Grading** — subgrade prep, base course, final grading for access roads, private drives, parking, and internal roadways; ditches and culverts.
5. **Retaining Walls** — boulder walls and engineered segmental-block walls for grade changes, slope stabilization, and erosion control.
6. **NCDOT / Public Infrastructure Work** — pre-qualified for NC Department of Transportation projects; code-compliant public-sector site work.
7. **Erosion & Sediment Control** — temporary and permanent measures kept in compliance with environmental/regulatory requirements.

*Supporting capabilities to mention where relevant:* septic system installation (per HomeAdvisor history), land clearing, demolition/debris removal, hauling (via Mason).
