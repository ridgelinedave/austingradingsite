Site Prep for a Growing Reems Creek Valley

Weaverville has grown from a quiet town north of Asheville into a sought-after place to build, spread through the rolling-to-steep ground of the Reems Creek valley. That growth brings a steady need for new building pads, driveways, and drainage, the unglamorous site work that has to be right before a single wall goes up.

From Burnsville we reach Weaverville in about forty minutes by way of US 19E and I-26. Whether it is a single custom home site or a handful of lots in a small development, we handle the full earthwork scope with one accountable crew so the dirt work does not become the thing that holds up the build.
