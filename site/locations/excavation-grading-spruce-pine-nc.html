In Mitchell County

Site Work Built for Mitchell County Rock

Spruce Pine anchors the southern end of Mitchell County along the North Toe River. The area is famous for what sits under the ground: this is the heart of the Spruce Pine mining district, home to some of the purest quartz and feldspar deposits found anywhere. For a homeowner or builder, that same geology means hard, mineral-rich subgrade and plenty of rock to cut before a pad is ever level.

We reach Spruce Pine in roughly thirty minutes from Burnsville by way of NC 226 and US 19E, close enough to run a full site-work scope without out-of-region mobilization dragging up the bill. When a lot near the Toe needs the rock ripped, the bank held, and the pad set, that is a normal day for our crew.
